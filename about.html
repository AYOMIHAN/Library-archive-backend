Our Mission

Why We Started

We all know the struggle: The semester starts, and suddenly you are chasing photocopies, begging seniors for past questions, or dealing with corrupted PDF files in WhatsApp groups.

It shouldn't be this hard to study.

That is why we built Campus e-Library. A centralized, clean, and free digital archive where materials are organized and just one click away. No ads, no broken links, just education.

100% Free

Education is a right. We don't charge for access to handouts or past questions.

Community Driven

Most of our materials are contributed by students and lecturers who want to help.

Fast & Secure

Hosted on modern cloud servers to ensure downloads are instant and virus-free.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this official?

We are an independent student initiative, created to support the academic community. We are not officially run by the school management.

How can I contribute materials?

We love contributors! If you have a clean PDF of a handout or past question, please contact us via the Contact Page.

Why can't I find my department?

We are constantly updating. If your department is missing, send us a request on WhatsApp and we will prioritize it.
